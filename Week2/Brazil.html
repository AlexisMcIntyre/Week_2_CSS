Brazil

Bom dia! I went on this trip with my boyfriend this summer to meet his family--they were truly delightful and hospitable people! We were in Montes Claros for the duration of the trip and it was a cute little city.

Fun Facts:

  • Fried tapioca might possibly be the best food on the face of the earth. Or in the galaxy.
  • A filet mignon and wine dinner for 2 costs $41 USD!! Feel like a boss on a budget! LOL
  • Eu quero tomar um cafezinho!!! Who would have thought Brazil would have the *cutest* little coffees (cafezinhos)?
  • A manicure *and* pedicure costs $5 USD. Yes, you read that right!
  • You don't actually have to be on time for anything.
  • There is no such thing as "bedtime".
  • Enjoy doing your laundry the old-fashioned way-by hand! Don't be fooled by the "machine"--it doesn't actually do anything. And don't forget your gloves!
  • There are 3 ingredients in every meal: beans, rice, meat. With a side of juice.
  • Ever wanted to know everything your neighbors are doing at all hours? Neither do I. But now you know.
